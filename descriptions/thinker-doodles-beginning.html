Thinker Doodles activities develop a wide variety of thinking skills as they engage your students in drawing and coloring. More fun than mindless coloring books, Thinker Doodles develop organized analysis and motor skills needed for success in reading, writing, and mathematics. These quick, fun activities can be used after school, at home, and on trips to stimulate developing minds.

These books give students visual clues and ask them to match complete pictures with unfinished forms. After they identify the pairs, they draw the missing parts to make matched sets. Students are then asked to follow directions and apply creative thinking to color the pictures.
